# MessyOS: Complete Product Blueprint

## Core Philosophy
**"Life is a mess, and we fix the fuck out of it to help you dive into what truly matters"**

A comprehensive life optimization platform that gamifies self-improvement while maintaining minimalistic, Web3-inspired aesthetics. Built for power users who want deep integration and automation rather than surface-level tracking.

---

## Technical Stack & Architecture

### Current Foundation
- **Frontend**: AstroJS + React components
- **Backend**: Supabase (Auth, Database, Real-time)
- **AI**: Gemini 2.5 Pro with LangChain/LangGraph
- **Deployment**: Vercel + Railway
- **Authentication**: Supabase Auth (moved away from Privy due to cost)

### Core System Features
- **Token Economy**: 4800 tokens for 30-day free trial
- **Mobile-First**: Progressive Web App with "Add to Home Screen"
- **Multi-User**: Customizable modules per user
- **Real-time**: Live updates and notifications
- **Privacy-Focused**: Strong encryption for sensitive data

## Cross-Module Integration for Perfect Day Planning

### How Modules Work Together for Optimal Scheduling

**Morning Routine Optimization:**
- **Health Module**: Sleep duration input → Energy level prediction
- **Calendar Module**: Class schedule (MSc at University of Birmingham) → Available time slots
- **Fitness Module**: Gym session planning with 3.7-mile cycling route optimization
- **Nutrition Module**: Breakfast timing (before/after gym) with macro planning
- **Task Module**: Assignment work scheduled in optimal energy windows

**Dynamic Decision Making:**
- **Classes at 11am**: Morning gym with cycling + shower time calculated
- **Classes at 9am**: Evening gym or train travel suggestion for time optimization
- **Weather Integration**: Rain → Suggest train travel instead of cycling
- **Energy Levels**: Low energy → Reschedule complex tasks, prioritize simple ones

**Meal Planning Integration:**
- **University Schedule**: Lunch at university (Deliveroo/dining options) vs home cooking
- **Nutrition Goals**: Macro targets influence meal choices and timing
- **Finance Module**: Cost optimization for meal decisions
- **Shopping Integration**: Amazon/grocery shopping scheduled based on ingredient needs

**Task Optimization:**
- **Habit Integration**: Daily cat litter cleaning, room cleaning automatically scheduled
- **Energy Matching**: Complex assignments during high-energy periods
- **Deadline Awareness**: Urgent tasks prioritized with buffer time
- **Location Context**: Grocery shopping near gym or university for route optimization

## Long-term Vision

MessyOS aims to become the definitive personal optimization platform - replacing the need for multiple habit trackers, expense apps, fitness apps, and productivity tools with one intelligent, integrated system that creates **perfectly planned days** from wake-up to sleep.

The platform should feel like having a personal life coach who knows every aspect of your life (sleep, classes, gym, meals, tasks, habits, health, finances) and creates optimized daily schedules that maximize productivity, health, and well-being while handling all the complex scheduling and optimization automatically.

**The goal is simple**: Wake up each morning to a perfectly planned day that considers your energy levels, class schedule, gym routine, meal requirements, task priorities, weather conditions, and local Birmingham context - all optimized for your success and well-being.
