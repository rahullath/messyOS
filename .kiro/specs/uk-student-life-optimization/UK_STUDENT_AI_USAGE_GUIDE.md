# UK Student AI Agent - Usage Guide

## Overview

The UK Student AI Agent (Mesh) is an intelligent conversational assistant specifically designed for Birmingham UK students. It understands your student lifestyle and can help with task management, schedule optimization, budget tracking, meal planning, and much more.

## Getting Started

### Accessing the AI Agent

1. Navigate to the UK Student Dashboard
2. Look for the "Mesh - UK Student AI" chat interface
3. Start typing your message or click a quick action button

### Quick Actions

The AI agent provides quick action buttons for common requests:

- **"I'm feeling tired"** - Get energy-based schedule adjustments
- **"Check my budget"** - See your spending analysis and budget status
- **"Plan my day"** - Generate a holistic daily plan
- **"What should I focus on?"** - Get priority recommendations

## Tips for Best Results

### 1. Be Specific
Instead of: "I'm busy"
Try: "I have a 9am class, gym at 5pm, and need to study for my essay"

### 2. Include Context
Instead of: "Create a task"
Try: "I need to submit my Corporate Finance assignment by 8/12"

### 3. Mention Constraints
Instead of: "Plan my day"
Try: "Plan my day - I have low energy, it's raining, and I have a 9am class"

### 4. Ask Follow-up Questions
The AI remembers your conversation, so you can ask follow-ups:
- "Can we move that to evening?"
- "What if I wake up at 7am instead?"
- "How much will that cost?"

### 5. Use Natural Language
The AI understands conversational language:
- "I'm knackered" = low energy
- "I'm skint" = low on money
- "I need to sort out my laundry" = schedule laundry
